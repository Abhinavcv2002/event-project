Welcome to Aura Event Management

We are an ISO 9001:2015-certified event management based in Kerala, with over 15 years of experience in the event management industry in Kerala.

Aura Event Management specializes in wedding event management, as well as a wide range of corporate, personal, regional, public, and private event management services—creating lasting memories for thousands of clients in the Malayalam community in Kerala and for those seeking destination weddings in Kerala from other states of India and abroad.

Our Story

Since 2010-2011, we have successfully organized over 350 weddings, 50 corporate events, 10 inaugurations, and 110 other private events. In 2025, we are committed to delivering 100 more exceptional events as we continue to grow and serve our clients with excellence.

Additionally, the brand name Aura Event Management is often referred to interchangeably as Aura, Event by Aura, or Aura Events by clients and customers. however, our official name remains Aura Event Management. We mainly serve in God's Own Country as a specialized event management for Kerala's event management services.

In 2008, our venture was initiated in Kuriachira, Thrissur. Initially, we handled event management tasks primarily in regions close to Thrissur and its surroundings. Our hard work has enabled us to expand from the neighborhoods of Thrissur to the popular territories of Cochin. Compared to other cities, we are now providing more services in Ernakulam (Kochi) through our own production house, a trend that has continued over the last five years. What started as a small firm has now expanded into a multi-facility event management service across Kerala.

We proudly serve all of Kerala, from bustling cities to serene villages, as one of the few Kerala-owned event management fully dedicated to the state. Deeply rooted in Kerala's rich culture and traditions, we specialize in creating unforgettable events—whether for the Malayalam community, on Kerala soil or destination weddings for guests from across India and beyond. With extensive experience in event management, we bring expertise and passion to every celebration.

Today, in 2025, Aura Event Management is strongly represented in Thrissur, Kozhikode (Calicut), Trivandrum, Kannur, Kollam, Alleppey (Alappuzha), Kottayam, Palakkad, Malappuram, and Kochi—the ten most dynamic cities of Kerala.

Our Vision

At Melodia Event Management, our vision is to become Kerala's leading event management company, dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for the Malayali community and people who come to host events like destination weddings in Kerala.

We aspire to redefine the event planning industry by offering innovative, creative, and highly personalized services, ensuring that every event, whether in cities, villages, or small towns, reflects the unique traditions and values of Kerala.

Our Mission

Our mission at Aura Event Management is to provide professional event planning and management services throughout Kerala, from the northern district of Kasaragod to the southern district of Trivandrum, including small cities, big cities, and villages.

We focus on delivering the best possible experience to our clients by combining traditional Kerala values with modern event management techniques, ensuring that each celebration is unique, memorable, and executed to perfection.

Our Journey

2008
